H.6_toy is a directory containing toy data (23.3 kreads) for practicing bioinformatic protocols in class.

In /data/ are 2 .fastq files from the made with Illumina Next-Seq PE (100x2) genome sequencing of a *Human herpesvirus* 6.

Also /clean/ contain trimmed read and /QC/ contain quality controls after and before trimming. 

In /assem/ and in  /annot/ are the the assembly and the annotation.
